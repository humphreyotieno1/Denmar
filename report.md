### Agreement Overview

This agreement outlines the terms and scope of social media content creation services provided by Humphrey Ouma to Harte Luxe Expeditions. Services will begin on **10-07-2026** at a rate of **KSh 4,500 per week**.

---

### Scope of Services

The Service Provider agrees to:

- **Create Promotional Posters:**  
  Showcasing the Client's tour packages, destinations, special offers, and travel updates.

- **Produce Short Promotional Videos/Reels:**  
  Developed and optimized for various social media platforms.

- **Curate Content & Provide Copywriting:**  
  Generating content that aligns with the Client's brand identity and effectively targets the desired audience.

- **Perform Content Revisions:**  
  Making reasonable adjustments to content per Client feedback, ensuring satisfaction with all deliverables.

---

### Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to:

- Supply timely, accurate details on tour packages, pricing, schedules, travel information, offers, and provide relevant media assets (photos or videos) to be featured in content.

---

### Workflow and Communication

- The Service Provider will convert the provided materials into engaging, publication-ready content for the Client’s social media channels.
- Both parties agree to ongoing communication for the review and approval of content, with a joint evaluation of content performance and workflow after the initial weeks to determine if adjustments are necessary.

---

### Payment Terms

- The fee for services is **KSh 4,500 per week**, payable at the end of each week, unless otherwise agreed in writing by both parties.

---

### Agreement Confirmation

By engaging in this service relationship, both parties agree to the terms outlined above.
